# Validation Checklist

Use this rubric when reviewing one live `projector` claim:

1. Read the exact claim text first.
2. Read the attached support body locally, not just the summary line.
3. Decide whether the support proves the exact claim or only a nearby behavior.
4. Check that the support boundary matches the claim boundary.
5. If the support is weak, tighten the verify or move the claim back to `@planned`.
